## 2024-07-22 - Icon-Only Button Accessibility in Rich Text Editors
**Learning:** Found that custom toolbars and rich text editors (`ForumRichText` and `PostLexicalEditor`) heavily relied on `title` attributes for tooltips but were missing explicit `aria-label` tags for screen readers. Using just `title` is often not sufficient for robust accessibility across different assistive technologies.
**Action:** When implementing custom toolbars or icon-only buttons, ensure that `aria-label` is always provided alongside `title` (or tooltip components) to guarantee accessible naming for screen reader users.

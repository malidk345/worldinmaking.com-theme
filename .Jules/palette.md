## 2024-05-18 - [Icon-only SVGs Keyboard Accessibility]
**Learning:** In this project, many interactive icon-only elements are raw SVGs with `onClick` handlers, rather than being wrapped in semantic `<button>` tags. Simply adding `role="button"` and `aria-label` is insufficient for accessibility; they also strictly require `tabIndex={0}` and an `onKeyDown` handler to support 'Enter' and Spacebar interactions for keyboard users, otherwise linting rules (like `jsx-a11y/interactive-supports-focus`) may fail or keyboard users will be blocked.
**Action:** Always ensure that when adding `role="button"` to non-semantic interactive elements, `tabIndex={0}` and keyboard event handlers are also implemented. For translated `aria-labels`, always reuse existing keys from the internal translation hook `t()`.
